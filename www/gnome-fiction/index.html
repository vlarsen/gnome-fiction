GNOME FICTION

GNOME FICTION is a social leveling and casual raiding guild.

We are found Alliance side, on the EU-Nagrand realm.

Guild Priorities

Leader: Caramint

The following guild achievement and tasks are currently top priority

  • Level a Jewel Crafter to 600, to get Working Better as a Team. This will unlock heirloom legs from the guild store.
  • Expanding number of factions with Exhalted status, working towards the guild achivement United Nations.
  • Get a rogue to lvl 85, for the A Class Act guild achievement, unlocking Shroud of Cooperation.

Achievement, Questing and Levelling Team

Leader: Cnack

Members: Lament, Tuányuán

The mission of the quest team, is to provide an environment for questing and discovery, to help people experience the Lore of WoW, discover and complete rewarding quest-chains, and earn achievements. Also to provide a social experience by having questing partners on comparable levels.

World Events

Going for the complete set of world events, awards the achievement What a Long, Strange Trip It's Been. We are currently doing Lunar Festival.

Raid Team

Leader: Monody

Members: Monody, Beastkitten/Fisticuffer, Glozd/Angramand, Váli

The raid team is currently working on the following:

  • Getting 10 actual people ready for normal mode raiding in Mogu'Shan Vaults.
  • Practicing encounters in LFR, and by interning with other raid teams.
  • Gearing up through heroic dungeons and rep-grinding Pandaria factions.
  • Establishing raid supplies in the guild bank
  • Testing various voice communication solutions to use during raiding

PvP Team

Members: Monody, Fisticuffer, Shikhome

PvP activity in the guild is currently experimental. Some people have started accumulating experience and gear, and participating is encouraged. Goals for the future is to participate from the start in season 13, and have people being able to form 2v2 and hopefully 3v3 teams to do arena.
